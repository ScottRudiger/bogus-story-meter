The Website Companion to the B.S.M. Extension

Bogus Story Meter is a web application and Chrome extension that allows users to rate webpages on their accuracy, in order to present an aggregate trustworthiness rating to all users. We hope that we can help others to 'spread the truth' and slow the spread of bogus information on the internet by making people more aware of the quality of what they're reading online.
