Welcome to Bogus Story Meter

The Website Companion to the B.S.M. Extension

Bogus Story Meter is a web application and Chrome extension that allows users to rate webpages on their accuracy, in order to present an aggregate trustoworthiness rating to all users. The motivation behind our project is to cut down on the proliferation of misinformation, urban legends, and otherwise baseless "facts" on the internet.
